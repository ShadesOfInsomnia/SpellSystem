Elemental Free 1.2.6
------------------------------------------------------------------

	Elemental Free contains 96 Shuriken Particles for desktop and mobile.

	This package is free version of Elemental Plus, more info please see https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/#!/content/76443

	Features:
	
		• Fire (23 prefabs).
		• Water (5 prefabs).
		• Wind (2 prefabs).
		• Earth (3 prefabs).
		• Thunder (5 prefabs).
		• Ice (4 prefabs).
		• Light (4 prefabs).
		• Darkness (2 prefabs).

		• Support all build player platforms.
		
	Compatible:

		• Unity 5.6.1 or higher.

Using demo scene
------------------------------------------------------------------

	1. Open Demo in "Elemental Free/Scenes/ELF Demo (960x600px)".
	2. Press Play button.
	3. Camera control
		Orbit:		mouse drag (single touch move on touch screen device).
		Zoom:		Mouse wheel (two fingers pinch/scroll on touch screen device).
	4. Particle test.
		Category:	Up/Down keys or click/tap on previous/next buttons.
		Particles:	Left/Right keys or click/tap on previous/next buttons.
